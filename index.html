What We Do

Five Core
Specialties

We provide technical expertise, guidance, and advice across five key practice areas — each designed to create measurable impact for your organisation.

01

Project Management

End-to-end project expertise from proposal to sustenance — ensuring quality, timeliness, and measurable results.

  • Project proposal development & preparation
  • Project planning & implementation
  • Project performance assessment
  • Risk assessment & management
  • Quality control & quality assurance
  • Monitoring, evaluation & reporting
  • Project consolidation & sustenance
02

Capacity Building & HR Development

Building organisations from within — through strategic human capital development and management solutions.

  • Strategic HR development & management
  • Organisational learning (including E-learning)
  • Leadership & front-line management
  • Reward & motivation strategy
  • Competency & talent management
  • Organisational change & transformation
  • HR planning, recruitment & selection
03

Organisational Health & Safety

Comprehensive health and safety frameworks that protect people, ensure compliance, and build a culture of care.

  • Health & safety policies & audits
  • Risk assessments & occupational health
  • Accident prevention & first aid policy
  • Employer H&S responsibilities
  • Hazard recognition & assessment
  • Disaster management
  • Emergency mitigation & preparedness
04

Research, Knowledge Brokering & Policy

Commissioned research and knowledge translation that connects organisations to evidence for sound public policy.

  • Exploratory, constructive & empirical research
  • Technical support for research organisations
  • Knowledge brokering & translation
  • Linkages between knowledge producers & users
  • Market insights & research evidence
  • Public policy development support

Community Engagement & Social Mobilisation

Bridging the gap between institutions and communities — fostering trust, participation, and grassroots-level development outcomes.

  • Community needs assessment & mapping
  • Stakeholder identification & mobilisation
  • Participatory planning & co-design
  • Social behaviour change communication (SBCC)
  • Community-based monitoring & feedback
  • Conflict resolution & consensus building
  • Civil society & NGO partnership facilitation
